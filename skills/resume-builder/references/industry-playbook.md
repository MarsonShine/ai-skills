# Industry Playbook for Resume Builder

Use this file when the user's role is domain-specific or when the source materials are too sparse and you need the right emphasis, vocabulary, and metric types.

## 1. Property, cleaning, hospitality, and frontline service

### Emphasize

- Team scheduling and attendance control
- Daily operations stability
- Quality inspection and standards enforcement
- Equipment use and upkeep
- Customer, tenant, or owner communication
- Complaint handling and turnaround
- Safety, hygiene, and service image

### Good metrics

- Team size or shift coverage
- Area / building / floor / room / zone coverage
- Inspection pass rate
- Complaint closure time
- Consumable cost reduction
- Onboarding or training speed

### Strong language patterns

- Coordinated multi-zone cleaning and staffing across public areas, back-of-house areas, or underground parking
- Established inspection and recheck routines to stabilize environment quality
- Organized shift assignments and rapid-response support during peak traffic, inspections, or events

## 2. Operations, project coordination, and administration

### Emphasize

- Cross-team coordination
- Schedule control
- SOPs and documentation
- Reporting cadence
- Vendor or stakeholder communication
- Resource allocation
- Issue tracking and closure

### Good metrics

- On-time completion rate
- Backlog reduction
- SLA or response time
- Process cycle time
- Budget adherence
- Meeting or reporting rhythm

### Strong language patterns

- Streamlined task assignment and follow-up to improve delivery consistency
- Built routine reporting and escalation loops that reduced issue carryover
- Coordinated stakeholders across departments to keep projects on schedule

## 3. Sales, business development, and customer success

### Emphasize

- Revenue or order growth
- Lead generation
- Account expansion
- Retention and renewal
- Negotiation and closing
- Pipeline discipline
- Customer relationship management

### Good metrics

- Revenue growth
- Target attainment
- Conversion rate
- Deal count or average order value
- Renewal rate
- Churn reduction

### Strong language patterns

- Expanded account coverage and improved follow-up cadence to lift conversion
- Built stable customer relationships that supported repeat purchases and renewals
- Managed pipeline visibility and prioritized higher-probability opportunities

## 4. Manufacturing, warehouse, logistics, and supply chain

### Emphasize

- Throughput and output stability
- Inventory control
- On-time delivery
- Safety discipline
- Equipment utilization
- Error reduction
- Shift or line coordination

### Good metrics

- Daily or weekly output
- Dispatch timeliness
- Inventory accuracy
- Defect reduction
- Downtime reduction
- Safety incident reduction

### Strong language patterns

- Coordinated personnel, materials, and equipment to keep operations stable during peak periods
- Improved inventory or dispatch accuracy through tighter checks and clearer handoffs
- Standardized work routines to reduce avoidable errors and delays

## 5. Finance, HR, and office support

### Emphasize

- Accuracy and compliance
- Confidential handling
- Payroll, hiring, or document processing
- Timeliness
- Cross-functional support
- Internal service quality

### Good metrics

- Error rate reduction
- Cycle time reduction
- Hiring fill time
- Payroll accuracy
- Attendance or documentation completeness

### Strong language patterns

- Maintained accurate records and process discipline under recurring deadlines
- Improved documentation completeness and turnaround through standardized templates
- Supported payroll, attendance, or hiring workflows with consistent accuracy

## 6. Technology, product, design, and digital roles

### Emphasize

- Products shipped
- User impact
- Reliability
- Performance
- Collaboration
- Experimentation and iteration

### Good metrics

- Feature adoption
- Conversion uplift
- Bug reduction
- Latency reduction
- Delivery speed
- Customer retention or NPS improvement

### Strong language patterns

- Shipped user-facing improvements that removed friction and improved adoption
- Reduced defects or technical issues through tighter review and test routines
- Translated business needs into practical execution with clear iteration cycles

## 7. Healthcare, education, and public service

### Emphasize

- Care quality or service quality
- Compliance and safety
- Throughput and responsiveness
- Stakeholder trust
- Record accuracy
- Program coordination

### Good metrics

- Service volume
- Satisfaction scores
- Compliance pass rate
- Response time
- Attendance or participation rate

### Strong language patterns

- Maintained high service standards while handling recurring volume and compliance requirements
- Coordinated people, schedules, and records to keep delivery orderly and reliable
- Improved communication and follow-up to strengthen user or stakeholder trust

## 8. If the industry is unclear

Fallback to universal strengths:

- reliability
- execution
- ownership
- coordination
- quality
- efficiency
- communication
- training
- problem-solving

Then quantify with the safest available evidence: scope, frequency, speed, quality, cost, and stability.
